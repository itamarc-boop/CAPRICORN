## Contact quality notes

- **Valid email** means the address is confirmed.
- **Catch-all** means the company domain accepts mail but the exact mailbox is not confirmed. Worth testing with a low-stakes first message.
- **LinkedIn only** means we have the person's name and title but not their email. You can reach them on LinkedIn or guess the company's email pattern.
- **No contact found** means Explorium has no person record for that company. Search by hand on LinkedIn or use the company website's contact form.

Of the 25 leads: 11 have a confirmed email, 6 have a catch-all email, 4 are LinkedIn only, and 4 have no contact (Textil Villa de Pego, Harpo seic, Geomembrane Bresciani, Sadovsky, Deppler and Brauchle).
